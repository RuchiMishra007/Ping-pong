AI PING PONG









Instructions

  1. Keep your laptop screen straight
  2. Move 3-4 feet away from the laptop
  3. Move your right wrist up or down, a red dot should appear on your wrist
  4. Press the play button, and move 3-4 feet away from laptop
  5. The red paddel is your paddel, that will move according to the movement of your right wrist
